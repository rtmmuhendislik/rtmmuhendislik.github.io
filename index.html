CV

I got my MSc. in Physics at Yale University, where I specialized in high-energy particle physics and researching the Higgs boson particle at the Large Hadron Collider at CERN.

My thesis work with the ATLAS Experiment at CERN involved the search for a rare interaction involving the Higgs boson decaying to a pair of tau particles.


I also implemented an RNN-based tau identification algorithm in the ATLAS high-level trigger software system.
